📜 Rules & Regulations

⏰ Time & Place

  • Assassinations may only occur during the school day (as shown on onBrooks)
  • Day ends at different times on Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays
  • No Saturday school = no Paranoia that day
  • Sleep-in periods are fair game

🛡️ Safe Zones

  • Classrooms (including Art Center)
  • Dormitories & Bathrooms
  • Chapel & Library
  • Dining Hall, Servery & Coffeehouse
  • Athletic Locker Rooms & Fitness Center
  • Busses to/from away games
  • Admissions Building & Health Center
  • School Meeting

⚠️ Danger Zones

  • Main Street
  • Outdoor fields & tennis courts
  • Hallways in academic & art buildings
  • Athletic Center
  • Danforth Building & The Portico
  • Student Center
  • Theatre (safe during school meeting block)
  • Boathouse

🔪 Elimination Rules

  • Tag must be from the back / out of sight
  • You must yell "WITNESSES" for the kill to count
  • 3 witnesses minimum must be present
  • Weapon must make physical contact
  • 5-second wait after eye contact or conversation
  • Submit the kill form immediately after

🛡️ The SHIELD

If you see your pursuer approaching, yell "SHIELD" — they cannot eliminate you for the rest of that day.

⚠️ ONE shield per day. Use it wisely.

🗡️ Weapons

  • Made of cardboard, tape, and/or hot glue only
  • NO pencils or other materials
  • Customization encouraged within material limits
  • Weapon must be in your hand touching the target

📖 The Matt Mulvey '21 Rule

You may not eliminate a player who has just delivered a Chapel Talk while they are standing outside of the Chapel receiving praise. You must wait until they are walking away.
